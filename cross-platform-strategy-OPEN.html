Why cross-platform vs KV1
KV1 — Current Vault
Drift-only · degraded since Oct 2025
  • Single-platform borrow: Drift only — hits capacity ceiling, can't scale
  • Negative funding: When Drift funding turns negative, hedge costs money
  • No routing: Stuck with whatever Drift offers regardless of market conditions
  • Negative carry: High borrow costs eating into JLP fee yield
  • Result: ~5% live APY vs 18% target — strategy degraded since Oct
Cross-Platform — New Strategy
Cobo + Voltr · 4 protocols · In development
  • Multi-source borrow: Drift + Hyperliquid + Aster — no single capacity constraint
  • Funding arbitrage: Routes borrows to platform with best rate at any moment
  • Dynamic routing: Voltr constantly rebalances to optimal conditions
  • Carry elimination: Borrow costs arbitraged away across platforms
  • Result: JLP fee yield compounds cleanly without carry drag — 33% backtest APY
