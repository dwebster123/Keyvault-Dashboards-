What is Sharpe Ratio?
Measures how much return you earn per unit of risk taken. A higher number means better returns with less volatility — the gold standard for institutional investment evaluation.
📊
3.0 = Exceptional
Most mutual funds and ETFs score between 0.5–1.5. Anything above 3.0 is considered exceptional by institutional standards and is rarely achieved consistently.
