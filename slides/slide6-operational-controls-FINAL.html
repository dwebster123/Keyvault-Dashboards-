Non-Custodial Custody
Smart contracts hold assets — not humans, not bank accounts.
Manager can trade but cannot withdraw. The vault smart contract enforces strict permissioning — fund managers execute trades within the vault but are cryptographically barred from moving investor capital to any external address.
Only you withdraw your funds. Redemptions are initiated directly by each investor via on-chain transaction — no intermediary, no approval queue, no counterparty risk in the exit process.
100% on-chain verifiable at all times. Every position, every trade, and every balance is publicly visible on Solscan. No black box — investors can independently verify holdings without relying on manager reporting.
Zero commingling of funds. Investor capital lives entirely within the vault smart contract and is never co-mingled with operating funds, manager accounts, or any third-party balance. Separation is enforced at the protocol level.
Permissioned role architecture. Vault roles — manager, depositor, administrator — each carry cryptographically scoped permissions. No single key can both trade and withdraw. A compromised manager key cannot access investor principal.
